I'm a Graphic Designer with over 8.5 years of experience in print production working with a B2B company while making files for in house and outsourced projects. Print Production

Made specialized files for digital printing (EPS / SVG / PSD / PDF), sending files out to vendors for ordering in tools and dies (EPS / DXF / PDF), and creating special files for all different types of machines and processes (Back-saved EPS & AI). Working with so many files, I created standard naming & organization strategies for all aspects of a project from file naming, resource listing, e-mail format and server backups.

Screen Printing

Out of flattened images, I had to create all screening separations for up to 5 different colors and test print to make sure everything would turn out right based on the halftone. If everything looked in order, I would then create filmwork to get shot in screens. My experience with a multitude of different art results in a clear understanding of how the screen printing process works and how to get the best outcome for both large and small scale products.

Design Work

Working and creating a basic understanding in several programs, I tried to push out different concepts of design work. Maya for 3-D renders learning framework and model limitations, InDesign for instruction booklets, Illustrator for clean edged design, and Photoshop for carrying a statid design theme. Only a small sample of my work so far, I look forward to larger projects with more driven experience in these programs to expand my sight and abilities over time.

Illustrations

Illustrated art has such a unique look that is crisp and clean. On projects I work on for both restoration or preparation for art, a clean high quality background stands out above everything else. A fun side project I work on regularly to keep my Illustrator skills brushed up for any project that comes my way.
